PDF Toolbox v1.Infinity

Drag an Office doc (DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, XLS, XLSX), TXT, RTF, or Image (BMP, JPEG, GIF, TIFF, PNG) to convert to PDF.

Drag a PDF here, select a format from a drop down, hit the button to send it and then it will stream to a download. (Note that JPEG/PNG output as a zip file.)
